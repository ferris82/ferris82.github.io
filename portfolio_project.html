1. PAPER KNIGHT QUEST - THE CUBE WORLD -

Paper Knight Quest: The Cube World is a thrilling mix of side-scrolling adventure and turn-based RPG battles. You’ll explore a colorful cube world, collect powerful heroes, and build unstoppable squads. Every journey through the semi-randomized dungeons brings new challenges, pushing you to strategize, adapt, and conquer floor after floor. Your ultimate quest for glory awaits!

THE EXPLORATION

Embrace 2-D platforming as a chilling adventure, and discover hidden treasure, traps and danger lurking beneath. Climb floor by floor to battle enemies and rescue lost wandering spirits. It is said the highest floor contains legendary treasures that are seeking among adventurers.

THE HEROES: PAPER KNIGHT

A wide variety of heroes with unique skills and play styles are available in Paper Knight Quests. With over 350+ unique characters and the Jobclass system, players can strategize and put together their ideal team in ways that suit their preferred gameplay style.style.

BUILD TEAMS: THE DREAM TEAM!

Build a main team and a backup team to synergy between the two, and defeat any challenging enemies.

THE ARENA: SHOW YOUR BRAVERY!

The Daily Arena Battle: Build your team and participate in battle with other AI-controlled players.

2. PAPER KNIGHT WARS

The realm of Paper Knight Wars is a magnificent military strategy game that invites players to conquer enormous regions with an arsenal of legendary heroes and mighty troops, and it is waiting for them to embark on an exciting adventure in its world. As players explore the world and take on its many quests, challenges, and strategic battles, they will be captivated by the game’s many exciting elements. The game features an impressive cast of over 50 unique heroes and troops, the ability to deploy three heroes at once, and the allure of joining clans to take on Raid Boss battles.
